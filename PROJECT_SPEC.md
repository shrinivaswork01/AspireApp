
# Aspire Studios - Architecture & Implementation Guide

## 1. App Overview
A premium SaaS CRM for photo studios. Designed with a focus on usability, aesthetic minimalism, and AI-powered automation.

## 2. Page-by-page Breakdown
- **Dashboard**: Unified view of KPIs, recent activity, and revenue.
- **Client Management**: CRM core for managing lead details and history.
- **Booking Workflow**: End-to-end event management from inquiry to delivery.
- **Package Builder**: Service catalog with tiered pricing.
- **Financial Tracker**: Ledger for payments and receivables.
- **Task Board**: Production management (Shooting, Editing, Post).
- **AI Studio**: Automated proposal writing, response generation, and business intelligence.
